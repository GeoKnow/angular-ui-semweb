Welcome

angular ui semweb is an attempt to assemble high quality angular.js directives for Semantic Web Applications.

This will include:

  • a label directive to provide nice and extensible label for humans
  • a tree directive to allow tree-based browsing of somehow connected resources (class tree, navigation tree, facet property tree, skos concept tree, geo hierarchy tree, ...)
  • a resource list directive to present tabular views of resources based on SPARQL queries
  • ...
